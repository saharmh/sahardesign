About

I'm Sahar Hosseini

The graphic designer and creative entrepreneur behind SaharDesign.

I’m a digital designer with over 10 years of experience of crafting online experiences for companies and brands. My goal is to help brands and products tell their stories in a way that’s both inspired and functional. From product and UX design, to user interfaces, to art direction, I can handle all aspects of the digital process.

I’ve worked with startup companies such as Strutta, QuickMobile, Work at Play and had the opportunity to work with some of the world’s top brands such as Microsoft, Salesforce, Google, Intel, Disney, Accenture, Sundance, TCM, MTV and Virgin Holidays.

Recognition: SmashingMagazine, Sitepoint
